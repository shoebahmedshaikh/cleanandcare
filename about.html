About Clean and Care Services

Our Story

Clean and Care Services was founded in Mumbai with a vision to provide top-quality cleaning solutions to both residential and commercial clients. With over 15 years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for excellence and reliability.

Our team of highly trained professionals is dedicated to ensuring your spaces are not just clean, but truly cared for. We use eco-friendly products and state-of-the-art equipment to deliver outstanding results while minimizing our environmental impact.
